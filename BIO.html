Biography

As a dedicated individual with a strong IT background, I began my journey in the disciplined environment of the United States Navy. Serving as an Information Systems Professional, I honed my technical skills and developed a deep appreciation for secure communication systems and the critical role of information technology. This six-year tenure equipped me with the skills and knowledge to transition into a Network Engineer role upon my honorable discharge.

After leaving the Navy, I faced a pivotal moment in my life. Drawing from my experience and the skills I had acquired, I decided to channel my technical expertise into the civilian world. This led me to the dynamic and ever-evolving field of Software Engineering. The transition was driven by my passion for problem-solving and the creative aspect of building software. I am particularly fascinated by how software engineering allows us to build complex systems that can solve real-world problems and improve people's lives.

As I continue to transition into this new career, I am committed to continuous learning and skill enhancement. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and am eager to contribute my unique blend of skills and experiences to the field of Software Engineering.
